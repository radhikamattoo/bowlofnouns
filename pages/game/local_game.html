Team 1

Gameplay and Settings:

1. Everyone's divided into 2 teams
2. Everyone writes down N (default 3) noun-words/phrases
3. Words are mixed up!
4. 3 rounds of gameplay, each round is 60 seconds rotating between teams until bowl is empty. Each team counts their solves words to keep score.
1st Round - Describe in away way, but cannot say the word.
2nd Round - 3 words only.
3rd Round - Charades.
Bonus Round - One sound only.
5. Team with the most points at the end of all rounds wins!
